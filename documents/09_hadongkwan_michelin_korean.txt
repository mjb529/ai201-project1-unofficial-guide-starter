Korean source material:
Michelin Korean says 하동관 has maintained a consistent reputation since 1939 and that its gomtang is clean but deep in flavor. Korean social snippets describe it as a representative gomtang house with 80+ years of history and multiple generations of operation.

Expanded guide text:
Hadongkwan is one of Seoul’s most important beef-soup institutions. It is a better local recommendation than many flashy modern restaurants because it is attached to a specific dish and a long urban memory. A user who asks for “old Seoul food” should hear about Hadongkwan. It is especially useful in Myeongdong, where many travelers are surrounded by tourist restaurants and need a historically meaningful alternative.

How to recommend:
- Recommend for gomtang, old Myeongdong, central lunch, no-nonsense Korean beef soup.
- Explain the difference from seolleongtang: gomtang is often clearer and beefier; seolleongtang is usually milkier from bones.
- Good for solo dining, breakfast/lunch, fast meals, and travelers who want one classic bowl.
