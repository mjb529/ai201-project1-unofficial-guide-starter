Korean source material:
Visit Korea Korean describes Euljiro alleys as including restaurants such as Yangmiok, kalguksu/mandu spots, and farther around Euljiro 4-ga, Woo Lae Oak with about 70 years of Pyongyang naengmyeon tradition and Munhwaok with about 60 years of seolleongtang tradition. The article also situates Seun Sangga as a 1968 mixed-use complex tied to Seoul’s older downtown commercial culture.

Expanded guide text:
This is the strongest activity source for the “local old Seoul” direction.
